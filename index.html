Welcome to discgolf putting tracker

This tool helps you track your putting accuracy across different distances.

  • Set Distance: Use the slider or arrows to pick your distance.
  • Unit: The range is 1–35 m (3–115 ft). The slider and image display distances only up to C2.
  • My Bag: Select how many discs you are throwing.
  • Missed: Log how many throws you missed.
  • Save: Click OK to calculate your success rate for that specific distance.
  • Data: Use the buttons at the bottom to download or upload your past sessions (json format). The reload will reset your progress.
  • Game Mode: Click the 🎮 button to enter game mode, where the distance changes every throw. You can set the min and max distance and total throws for the game session.
  • Support: If you would like to support this project, please consider supporting us via PayPal
